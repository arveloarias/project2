Personnel Department Mission

The personnel department is the department that deals with matters involving employees, such as hiring, training, labor relations, and benefits. Also, the personnel department makes the function of the ministry responsible for the management and motivation of people in the workplace in order for them to be productive. This department functions in hiring, using, and maintaining a satisfied workforce.
